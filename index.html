     Singing is my way of expressing my feelings, usually through other people's words; because the songs I sing aren't mine. They were written by someone else and the songs are how the song writers describe their feelings. I do wish someday I can express my feelings in my own words though. Whenever I discover a catchy song or well written/produced song, I get so hyped up about writing and producing my own song. The hype usually dies off after a few days but during those few days, I would get so hopeful that I will learn to write and produce. If only I have the skills and knowledge to do so, even if my songs aren't good, I'd still be so incredibly satisfied.

No goal, no motivation, no energy left to do anything. That is how I have been feeling for the past week after finishing all of my final exams for Spring 2021. It is not that because I have nothing left to do or no goal to work towards. I do keep track of all the things I need to do but right now, they are nothing more than descriptions of a responsible adult. It's funny how I felt so frustrated while I was busy studying and didn't have time to do all the things on the list but now that I do have time to do them, I just don't feel like doing anymore. Is this what burned out feels like?
